Dear Anna,

I am sorry but your grandmother, Margaret, passed away last night.

She lived alone, so the authorities asked me to get ahold of you.

We may have only been neighbours, but she was a good woman.

She talked about you (her “sweet pea”, she would say) and

how you were doing in the big city often.

I have the keys to her property at 9812 Beech Road whenever you need it.

My condolences,

Frank

When I was 5, I gave this drawing to my grandma as a birthday present. We lived in a house that was falling apart, but I guess it never felt that way when she was here.
I'm finally coming home, grandma.
